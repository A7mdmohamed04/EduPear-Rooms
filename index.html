About EduPear Rooms

EduPear Rooms is a platform designed to facilitate collaborative learning and peer-to-peer knowledge sharing. Our mission is to make education more accessible, interactive, and engaging through technology.

Built on the powerful PeerJS library, our platform enables direct, efficient connections between users without unnecessary server overhead.
